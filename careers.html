Performance Marketing
Meta Ads Manager

You'll run and scale Meta Ads campaigns for our UK auto styling clients — window tinters, wrappers, detailers. You'll own the numbers: cost per lead, ROAS, conversion rates. If campaigns aren't performing, you diagnose and fix fast. This is a hands-on, high-ownership role.

Must have: Proven Meta Ads experience, understanding of direct-response advertising, ability to read data and optimise fast, strong creative instincts.

Sales & Client Success
Lead Follow-Up Closer / Client Account Manager

You'll be turning warm leads into booked appointments for our clients — and keeping those clients happy, retained, and scaling. Half closer, half relationship manager. Natural on the phone, confident handling objections, and genuinely good with people.

Must have: Strong sales and communication skills, comfortable on the phone, organised follow-up, ability to handle objections without sounding scripted. Sales background in automotive, trades, or local services is a massive advantage.
Big plus: If a lead asks whether PPF is worth it on a leased car, you can actually have that conversation. If a client mentions ceramic tint vs dyed film, you know what they mean. Automotive knowledge turns good salespeople into great ones in this niche.
