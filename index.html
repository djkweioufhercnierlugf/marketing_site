Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

What makes Meta ads better than Google or YouTube ads for auto styling businesses?
Meta ads allow us to target customers based on their interests, behaviors, and demographics with incredible precision. Unlike Google ads where people are actively searching (meaning high competition), Meta ads put your business in front of potential customers before they even start looking - at a much lower cost per lead. It's an untapped platform in the auto styling niche with huge opportunities.
Do you only work with window tinters and auto stylers?
Yes, absolutely. We specialize exclusively in window tinting and auto styling businesses in the UK and US. This narrow focus means we understand your industry inside and out - the customer journey, pricing expectations, seasonal trends, and what messaging converts. We don't dilute our expertise by working with other industries.
How quickly can I expect to see results?
Most clients start seeing qualified leads within the first 7-14 days of launching campaigns. However, we optimize continuously, and results typically improve significantly over the first 60-90 days as we refine targeting and messaging based on real data from your market.
How much should I budget for Meta ads?
We typically recommend a minimum ad spend of £500-£2,000 per month to gather sufficient data and generate meaningful results. However, the exact amount depends on your market, goals, and capacity. During your strategy call, we'll discuss a budget that makes sense for your specific situation.
Do I need to handle the leads myself?
We can handle lead management for most clients with our sales team and in-house systems, or we can provide qualified leads directly to you. The choice is yours based on your preference and capacity.
